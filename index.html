Our Sustainable Process

From collection to your doorstep, we ensure every step minimizes environmental impact.

1

Material Collection

We partner with local recycling centers and businesses to collect used cardboard, diverting tons of waste from landfills each month.

2

Cleaning & Processing

Collected materials are thoroughly cleaned and processed using eco-friendly methods to remove contaminants and prepare for pulping.

3

Pulping & Sheet Formation

The cleaned cardboard is mixed with water to create pulp, which is then formed into new sheets using energy-efficient machinery.

4

Corrugation & Cutting

Sheets are corrugated for strength and precision-cut to customer specifications with minimal material waste.

5

Sustainable Shipping

Finished products are shipped using carbon-neutral logistics to businesses committed to sustainable packaging solutions.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Founded in 2015, King & Christ began with a simple mission: to create packaging solutions that don't compromise our planet's future. What started as a small recycling operation has grown into a leading provider of sustainable cardboard products.

We believe that businesses shouldn't have to choose between quality packaging and environmental responsibility. That's why we've invested in innovative recycling technologies and sustainable practices that allow us to deliver both.

By choosing King & Christ, you're helping to:

  • Reduce deforestation and preserve natural habitats
  • Divert tons of waste from landfills each year
  • Support a circular economy that values reuse
  • Lower carbon emissions through local production
